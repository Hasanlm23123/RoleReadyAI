Resume-to-Interview Studio

Paste a resume and job post, then turn that into an actual recruiting plan.

RoleReady AI scores candidate fit, surfaces strengths and gaps, rewrites bullets, drafts a short recruiter pitch, and builds interview prep questions from the same inputs. It is designed as a Vercel-ready full-stack app with a static frontend and a server-side Groq route.
